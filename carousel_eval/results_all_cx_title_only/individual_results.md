## Composite Score Methodology

The **composite score** is computed per (consumer, daypart) group as a weighted average of available metrics, with weights renormalized when metrics are missing:

- **Weights**: MMS 20%, SR@5 15%, CCR 15%, ILD 10%, OHCD 10%, TMC 15%, FCS 15%
- **Dayparts with orders**: All 7 metrics contribute to composite (full weights)
- **Dayparts without orders** (marked `[NO ORDERS]`): Only ILD (10%), TMC (15%), and FCS (15%) are available. These weights are renormalized to sum to 1.0 (ILD 25%, TMC 37.5%, FCS 37.5%), so the composite reflects only carousel quality — not relevance.
- **Summary table** averages composite scores across **only dayparts with orders**, since those composites use the full metric set and are directly comparable.
